## Search Parameters

When executing Exa searches:

- **Date range:** Last 14-30 days (prefer recent, fall back to 30 days if insufficient results)
- **Queries per run:** 3-5 (rotate across pillars for diversity)
- **Results per query:** Top 5-10 results
- **Filter:** English language, reputable sources (business publications, tech media, research reports, government announcements)
- **Minimum viable results:** 8 angles total. If Exa returns fewer than 8 usable angles across all queries, supplement with evergreen angles below

## Evergreen BOFU Angles (Fallback)

Use these when the news cycle is quiet or Exa returns insufficient current results. Each is a proven BOFU angle that works regardless of timing.

### SaaS Waste Evergreens

1. **"The average SME pays $5K/mo for tools that don't talk to each other"** — Universal pain, always relevant. Lead with the dollar figure, show the typical stack cost breakdown
2. **"Every new hire costs you $200/mo in SaaS before they do anything useful"** — Per-user pricing expose. Scale the math: 10 hires = $24K/year in new SaaS fees alone
3. **"You're renting your business infrastructure from 15 different landlords"** — Vendor lock-in metaphor. Contrast with ownership model

### Efficiency Evergreens

4. **"Your best employees spend 60% of their day on work that should be automated"** — Context switching and manual process waste. Use the 15-25 hrs/week stat
5. **"One wrong data entry field cost this business $10K"** — {CASE_STUDY_2} case study angle. Single-entry systems prevent errors
6. **"The tools that got you to $1M will break you at $5M"** — Growth-phase systems pain. Agricultural Equipment / {CASE_STUDY_1} case studies

### AI Enablement Evergreens

7. **"58% of employees have never used AI at work — and it's not their fault"** — AI blindness angle. The barrier is data, not willingness
8. **"AI doesn't work on chaos — here's what you need first"** — Data foundation prerequisite. Unified system enables AI; scattered tools prevent it
9. **"You can now ask your business data any question in plain English"** — RAG/semantic search demo angle. Show the possibility, bridge to audit

### Case Study / Social Proof Evergreens

10. **"We purchased a $200K business and hit $1M in 6 months — here's the operating system"** — [YOUR_STORY: replace with a real business transformation case study]. Systems injection story
11. **"40 staff, $18M revenue, and every contract was on paper"** — {CASE_STUDY_1} case study. Scale + manual process contrast
12. **"Custom software used to cost $1M. AI dropped that by 50x."** — Market timing angle. The math has flipped — custom is now cheaper than annual SaaS
