## Sentence Structure Rules

- **60/40 split:** 60% longer flowing sentences, 40% short punchy statements
- **Average:** ~20 words per sentence
- **Momentum builder:** Cascading "and" lists to build energy
- **Impact technique:** Short declarative for emphasis: "This is not a scare campaign. It's a genuine wakeup call."
- **Adverbial qualifiers:** Heavy use of "actually", "really", "exactly" — these are Adam's verbal signature

---

## Credibility Delivery Style

- **Tone:** Matter-of-fact, never boastful
- **Delivery:** Single sentence, rattle off the numbers — no setup line, no "let me explain why I'm qualified"
- **Example:** "I'm top 1% on Upwork, around $1M on the platform publicly, and over $1M in revenue as an AI agency."
- **Numbers:** Stack 2-3 stats in one breath — Upwork rank, revenue, project count
- **Duration:** ~5-8 seconds max, then move on immediately
- **Self-aware exit:** "with all the boring stuff out of the way" after credentials

---

## Tone & Energy Profile

- Calm authority — NOT hype, NOT clickbait
- Australian conversational: "look", "so", contracted language throughout
- Respects viewer time explicitly ("without wasting any more of your time")
- Zero clickbait language: no "shocking!", "revealed!", "exposed!"

## Contractions (Mandatory)

Always use contractions. Adam never speaks formally.

| Always | Never |
|--------|-------|

| you're | you are |
| we're | we are |
| I'm gonna | I am going to |
| don't | do not |

## Direct Address Pattern

- Use "you" roughly every 2-3 sentences
- Pattern: "If you [condition], then this video is for you"
- Use inclusive "we" language when walking through builds

## What Adam NEVER Does

- No clickbait emotional language
- No apologies or hedging expertise
- No generic openers ("hey guys", "what's up everyone")
