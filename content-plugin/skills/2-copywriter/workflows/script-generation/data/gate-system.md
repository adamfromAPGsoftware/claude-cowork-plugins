# Gate System — Content Validation Framework

Reference validation framework for evaluating content ideas before script generation. Extracted from empirical content strategy patterns.

---

## Gate System

Every content idea runs through 6 generative gates. Each gate validates AND produces output simultaneously. The process IS the deliverable.

### Gate 1: ICP Check
- Confirm audience alignment against loaded ICP profiles
- Draft the audience angle
- Pass criteria: idea clearly serves builders/learners AND/OR SME decision-makers

### Gate 2: Value Clarity
- Confirm ROI or value proposition is obvious
- Draft the promise statement
- Pass criteria: viewer can immediately see time saved, money made, or skill gained

### Gate 3: Before/After
- Validate the transformation structure
- Draft body outline: current process > what we build > new process
- Pass criteria: clear, demonstrable transformation that inherently shows ROI

### Gate 4: Hook Test
- Validate hook strength using 5 reference-derived hook patterns:
  - **Number + bold claim**: Lead with the most impressive metric — "$400K last month...", "$1000 online with AI"
  - **Listicle + value tag**: "Here are [N] [adjective] [things] for [value]" — "5 boring AI automations for $1500 a pop"
  - **Compression promise**: "[Large scope] in just [small time]" — "6 years of prompt engineering in 53 minutes"
  - **Contrarian twist**: "[Impressive claim]... [surprising reversal]" — "$400K last month... worst career move"
  - **Direct promise**: "I'm going to show you [X]" + power statement (client result / personal implementation / business metric)
- Generate 3 hook options using different patterns from above
- Every hook MUST include a specific number and complete within 5 seconds of speech
- Include a credibility stacking plan for seconds 5-10 (what proof is shown immediately after the hook)
- Pass criteria: at least one hook that would stop a scroll, includes a specific number, and has a credibility follow-up

### Gate 5: Dual-Funnel
- Score for TOFU: does this teach builders a sellable/usable skill?
- Score for BOFU: does this demonstrate ROI to SME decision-makers?
- Refine positioning to maximise both
- Pass criteria: scores positively on at least one, ideally both

### Gate 6: Scope Guard
- Confirm idea can be delivered concisely in one video
- Check for scope creep: "You've got three videos in one idea"
- Lock the final brief
- Pass criteria: single clear message, deliverable without padding

---

## Adaptive Intensity

- **Strong logline** (clear WHO/WHAT/WHY): Fast-track through gates, light validation, heavy output generation
- **Medium logline** (has potential but gaps): Full gate treatment, deep refinement at weak points
- **Weak logline** (can't articulate clearly): Flag early — "This logline isn't landing. Here's why. Pivot or kill?"

---

## Kill/Sharpen Decision

After gates, one of three outcomes:
1. **Strong** — Proceed with script generation
2. **Medium** — Sharpen at the specific gates that failed, then proceed
3. **Weak** — Kill with clear reasoning. "This doesn't hit your ICP. Here's specifically why. Want to rework or bring me something else?"
