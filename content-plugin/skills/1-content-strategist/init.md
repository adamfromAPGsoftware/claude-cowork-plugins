# First-Run Setup for CCS Content Strategist

Welcome! Setting up your workspace.

## Memory Location

Creating `{project-root}/content-plugin/data/memory/1-content-strategist-sidecar/` for persistent memory.

## Initial Structure

Creating:
- `index.md` — active projects, research status, current work
- `patterns.md` — research patterns, trending topic clusters, successful ideation approaches
- `chronology.md` — session timeline

## Setup Questions

1. **Content niche** — What's your primary content niche/space?
   *(e.g. AI/tech education, SaaS, agency, etc.)*

2. **Target platforms** — Which platforms are you actively publishing to?
   *(e.g. YouTube, LinkedIn, X, Instagram, TikTok, Blog, Email)*

3. **Competitor channels** — List 3-5 competitor YouTube channels to track.
   *(Channel name and URL or @handle)*
