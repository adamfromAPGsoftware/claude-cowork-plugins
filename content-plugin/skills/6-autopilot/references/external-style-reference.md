# Sample Creator LinkedIn Style Guide

> Distilled from 9 analysed posts in the inspiration library (`memory/2-copywriter-sidecar/inspiration/linkedin.md`).
> Sample Creator is the primary LinkedIn style reference — similar audience, similar niche (AI automation), proven reach.

---

## Format Patterns

- **Image posts dominate** — 9/9 analyzed posts use images. Images with bold statements or frameworks outperform text-only by 2-3x for his account.
- **Short posts** — most posts are under 400 words. Density over length.
- **Single idea per post** — one clear takeaway, not a roundup.

---

## Hook Patterns (by frequency)

| Hook Type | Example | Why It Works |
|-----------|---------|--------------|
| Social proof + specific number | "I've helped over 2,000 people get their first AI client" | Establishes authority + implies a path |
| Income proof + timeframe | "I hit $25,000/month in late 2023" | Aspirational anchor, specific |
| Named framework teaser | "Here's the 3-step framework I use to..." | Curiosity + implied system |
| Client retention stat | "My clients stick around for 2+ years on average" | Proof of quality without bragging |

---

## Body Structure

**Lead magnet posts:**
1. Hook (1-2 lines)
2. Reason to believe / justification (3-5 bullet points, `-` dashes, not `→` arrows)
3. Lead magnet teaser — name the resource and what it gives them
4. 2-step CTA: "1. Like this post  2. Comment [KEYWORD]  3. I'll DM you"
5. (Optional) P.S. connection request

**Personal/nurture posts:**
1. Hook statement (bold claim or personal fact)
2. Brief narrative or explanation (2-3 short paragraphs)
3. Takeaway line or principle
4. No CTA — let the post breathe

---

## Tone Calibration

- **Direct authority, not guru hype.** Sample Creator states things as facts, not as "here's my opinion."
- **Vulnerable when earned.** Shares failures and setbacks, but always with a lesson or outcome.
- **Short sentences.** Never academic. Never corporate.
- **No buzzwords.** "Leverage", "ecosystem", "synergy" don't appear. Simple language, complex ideas.
- **No hashtags.** None in any analyzed post.

---

## Visual Style (for image posts)

- Clean gradient backgrounds (dark to slightly lighter — blue/grey tones in his brand)
- Bold single statement as headline text (max 8-10 words)
- No complex layouts — one message, big text
- Income numbers or specific stats rendered as large text anchors
- No creator in the image — pure statement cards

**Creator's adaptation:** Same simplicity principle, but use the brand colours (`{brand.colors.primary}` from config.yaml, dark backgrounds) instead of Sample Creator's blue tones. Tool logos or screenshots instead of pure text cards where possible.

---

## CTA Mechanics

- **2-step CTA** (not 3-step): "1. Like  2. Comment [KEYWORD]" — Sample Creator doesn't always include the connect step
- **The creator uses 3-step** (like + comment + connect) which is proven to outperform 2-step for DM delivery
- **ALL-CAPS keywords** like "ROADMAP", "PRICING", "COURSE" — never lowercase
- **Short resource descriptions** — "the exact roadmap I followed" not "a comprehensive guide covering..."

---

## What NOT to copy

- Sample Creator's colour palette (blue/grey tones) — the creator's brand is set in config.yaml
- The explicit "Want it?" hook before the CTA — can feel manipulative
- Purely image-card format for every post — the creator does text-heavy posts that perform better for lead magnets
