# Spend Category Taxonomy

## Standard Categories

| ID | Label | Description | Example Merchants |
|----|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| software | Software & Subscriptions | SaaS tools, licenses, API usage | Anthropic, OpenAI, GitHub, Figma, Slack |
| hosting | Hosting & Infrastructure | Cloud hosting, CDN, DNS, domains | AWS, Cloudflare, Vercel, DigitalOcean |
| contractors | Contractors & Freelancers | Outsourced dev, design, consulting | Fiverr, Upwork, direct contractor payments |
| travel | Travel & Transport | Flights, rideshare, fuel, parking | Uber, Qantas, Shell, Wilson Parking |
| meals | Meals & Entertainment | Client meals, team lunches, coffee | Restaurants, UberEats, DoorDash |
| office | Office & Equipment | Hardware, supplies, furniture | Apple, JB Hi-Fi, Officeworks |
| marketing | Marketing & Advertising | Ads, content, sponsorships | Google Ads, Meta, LinkedIn |
| professional | Professional Services | Legal, accounting, insurance | Accountant, law firm, insurance provider |
| other | Other | Uncategorized or miscellaneous | Anything that doesn't fit above |

## Notes

- MCC codes are a starting point, not absolute — some merchants have misleading codes
- Merchant name is usually more reliable than MCC for categorization
- When both conflict, prefer merchant name match over MCC
