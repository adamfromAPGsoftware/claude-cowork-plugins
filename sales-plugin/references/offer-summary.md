## Discovery Call Structure (30 min)

### 1. Warm-Up (3 min)
- Ask where they're based, their role, how they found you
- Permission statement: "Can I ask a few questions about how your business runs before I show you anything?"

### 2. Pain Discovery (18 min) — LISTEN MORE THAN TALK
Lead with your primary value pillar — one or two questions per pillar, listen hard:
- "{YOUR_DISCOVERY_QUESTION_1}"
- "{YOUR_DISCOVERY_QUESTION_2}"

**Target talk ratio**: 60% prospect / 40% you. If you're talking more than 40%, ask another question.

### 3. ROI Sketch (5 min)
- Quick math: "Based on what you told me — [X employees], losing roughly [Y hrs/week per person] — that's conservatively [$Z/year] in waste"
- Write the numbers out loud using their specific figures

### 4. Close to Entry Product (4 min)
- "The next step isn't to jump straight into a build — that wouldn't be fair to you. What we do first is {YOUR_ENTRY_PRODUCT}..."
- Walk them through deliverables
- State the price, guarantee, and credit terms
- "I'll send the invoice today."

## Objection Handlers

Framework: **LISTEN -> VALIDATE -> REFRAME -> BRIDGE**

### 1. "The price is too high"
- **Validate**: "I get it — this is a significant investment."
- **Reframe**: "Let's look at the math. You're spending [$X/year] on tools this replaces, and your team is losing [$Y/year] in inefficiency."
- **Bridge**: "{YOUR_GUARANTEE_BRIDGE}"

### 2. "What if it doesn't work for us?"
- **Validate**: "That's a smart concern."
- **Reframe**: "That's exactly why we start with {YOUR_ENTRY_PRODUCT} — not a full build. {YOUR_GUARANTEE_TERMS}."
- **Bridge**: "We're confident enough in the ROI to back it with our guarantee."

### 3. "We already use [Competitor/Tool]"
- **Validate**: "That's great — you've already invested in improving processes."
- **Discovery**: "How is [Competitor] working? Any friction points?"
- **Reframe**: "[Competitor] handles [narrow use case] but you're still juggling [X other tools] for the rest."

### 4. "Our team won't adopt another new tool"
- **Validate**: "Change fatigue is real — I hear that a lot."
- **Reframe**: "We're not adding a tool — we're removing tools. Instead of logging into 5+ platforms, they have one login, one dashboard."

### 5. "We need to talk to [partner/board] first"
- **Validate**: "This should absolutely be a team decision."
- **Facilitate**: "What information would help that conversation? I can put together a summary."
- **Close**: "Let's schedule a follow-up — does [date] work?"
