## 3. Phase 2: Sprint-Based Implementation

**Per sprint**: $14,999
**Sprint duration**: 2 weeks
**Typical engagement**: 3-10 sprints ($44,997-$149,990)
**Resource per sprint**: 80 hrs development + 10 hrs project management

### Sprint cycle

Each sprint follows a consistent rhythm:

```
Week 1, Monday    — Sprint kickoff: goals, priorities, acceptance criteria confirmed
Week 1, Thursday  — Check-in: progress update, any blockers surfaced early
Week 2, Monday    — Mid-sprint review: first working features ready for feedback
Week 2, Thursday  — Sprint close: deploy, demo, retrospective, next sprint planning
```

You have a dedicated point of contact throughout. No ticket queues, no support delays.

### The 80-90% rule

We build to 80-90% completion each sprint and deploy. The remaining polish is handled in parallel with the next sprint — not held up waiting for perfection. This keeps you shipping real functionality every two weeks instead of waiting months for a "complete" system.

## 8. R&D Tax Incentive

If your company turns over **under $20M/year**, you may be eligible for the Australian Government's R&D Tax Incentive — returning **43.5 cents in every dollar** spent on eligible R&D activities.

Custom AI and software development typically qualifies. This effectively reduces your build cost by nearly half.

**Example**: $89,994 in sprints (6 sprints) → ~$39,147 returned via R&D tax offset → **net cost ~$50,847**

We provide documentation to support your R&D claim. Speak to your accountant to confirm eligibility.
