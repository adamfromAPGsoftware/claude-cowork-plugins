## 4. Pricing and Payment

### Pricing Models

4.1. Fees for each SOW will be structured under one of the following models, as specified in the SOW:

**(a) Fixed Price** — A total fixed price for the defined scope of work, payable in milestone instalments as set out in the SOW. Unless otherwise specified in the SOW:
- 50% of the total Fees are due upon execution of the SOW ("Deposit");
- Remaining Fees are due upon completion of each milestone as defined in the SOW.

**(b) Sprint-Based** — A fixed price per Sprint (14-calendar-day cycle), invoiced at the commencement of each Sprint. The scope of each Sprint is agreed between the Parties at the start of that Sprint. Unless otherwise specified:
- The first Sprint Fee is due upon execution of the SOW;
- Subsequent Sprint Fees are due at the commencement of each Sprint.

### Payment Terms

4.2. All Fees are in Australian Dollars (AUD) and exclusive of GST. GST will be added where applicable in accordance with the *A New Tax System (Goods and Services Tax) Act 1999* (Cth).

4.3. Invoices are payable within 7 days of the invoice date unless otherwise specified in the SOW.

4.4. If any invoice remains unpaid for more than 14 days after the due date:
- (a) may suspend performance of all Services under the relevant SOW (and any other SOW at its discretion) by giving written notice to the Client;
- (b) interest will accrue on the overdue amount at the Reserve Bank of Australia cash rate plus 2% per annum, calculated daily from the due date until paid in full;
- (c) the Client is liable for all reasonable costs incurred by {YOUR_COMPANY} in recovering overdue amounts, including legal fees and collection costs.

4.5. may resume suspended Services within a reasonable time after all overdue amounts (including accrued interest) are paid in full.

4.6. Fees set out in a SOW may only be varied by a Change Request or a new SOW signed by both Parties.

## 12. Termination

### Termination for Convenience

12.1. **Sprint-Based SOWs:** Either Party may terminate a sprint-based SOW by giving the other Party at least 14 days' written notice. Termination takes effect at the end of the current Sprint or 14 days after notice, whichever is later.

12.2. **Fixed-Price SOWs:** Either Party may terminate a fixed-price SOW by giving the other Party at least 30 days' written notice. Termination takes effect at the end of the current milestone period or 30 days after notice, whichever is later.

### Termination for Cause

12.3. Either Party may terminate this Agreement or any SOW immediately by written notice if:
- (a) the other Party commits a material breach of this Agreement and fails to remedy that breach within 14 days of receiving written notice specifying the breach; or
- (b) the other Party becomes insolvent, enters into administration, liquidation, or receivership, or ceases (or threatens to cease) carrying on business.

### Consequences of Termination

12.4. Upon termination of a SOW or this Agreement:
- (a) the Client must pay for all Services performed and expenses incurred up to the effective date of termination, including any work-in-progress in the current Sprint or milestone;
- (b) will deliver to the Client all completed Deliverables and work-in-progress for which payment has been received, in their then-current state;
- (c) each Party must return or destroy the other Party's Confidential Information in accordance with clause 6;
- (d) data handling will be in accordance with clause 7.6.

12.5. For fixed-price SOWs, if the Client terminates for convenience, the Deposit is non-refundable.

### Survival

12.6. The following clauses survive termination of this Agreement: clauses 1 (Definitions), 4 (Payment — to the extent of outstanding obligations), 5 (Intellectual Property), 6 (Confidentiality), 7.6 (Data on Termination), 9.3–9.7 (Disclaimers), 10 (Limitation of Liability), 11 (Indemnification), 14 (Dispute Resolution), and 15 (General Provisions).
