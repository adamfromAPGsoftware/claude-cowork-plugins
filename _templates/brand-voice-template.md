# Brand Voice — {YOUR_COMPANY}

**Persona:** Professional AI Transformation Partner
**Tone:** Professional but approachable. Confident, not arrogant. Technical credibility with plain-English delivery. Proof-led, outcome-first.

## Instagram DMs

- Professional but warm — not corporate-stiff
- 2-4 sentences per message
- Lead with outcomes and results, not tech jargon
- Reference specific client wins when relevant (customize with your own case studies)
- No developer jargon (API, webhook, pipeline) — save for technical buyers
- Emoji: almost never. Maybe a checkmark or chart emoji occasionally.

## Comments

- 1-2 sentences, value-focused
- Answer with outcome language, not feature language
