## Section 9: Key Patterns & Takeaways

1. **The "Infinite Canvas" Strategy:** The core educational content relies on a digital whiteboard. Instead of cutting between static slides, the video pans and zooms across a large canvas, keeping the visual field dynamic without requiring complex post-production edits.
2. **Context-Aware PiP Positioning:** The circular PiP (~10-12% width) is not static; it moves between the bottom-right and top-left corners depending on where the crucial information is on the screen share.
3. **Hard Cuts Only:** Zero use of cross-dissolves, wipes, or fades. Every transition is a hard cut, maintaining a crisp, fast-paced feel.
4. **Jump-Cut Zooms for Pacing:** During full-frame speaker segments, slight punch-ins (~1.05x scale) are used to hide edits and add subtle visual resets.
5. **Bold, Transient Text Overlays:** Text added over the speaker is large, bold, sans-serif, and appears/disappears instantly to emphasize specific keywords or statistics.
6. **Zero Audio Distractions:** No background music or sound effects. The instructional voiceover is the sole audio element.
7. **Speaker as the Anchor:** The full-frame speaker is used to hook the viewer, transition between major macro-sections, and deliver high-impact statements, while the PiP is used for the actual teaching.
8. **No Burned-in Captions:** The video relies entirely on the visual aids (whiteboard/slides) and the clarity of the voiceover.

## Section 10: Remotion Implementation Notes

- **PiP Implementation:**
  - Use a perfect circle: `borderRadius: '50%'`, `overflow: 'hidden'`.
  - Size: `width: '12vw'`, `height: '12vw'` (assuming 16:9 composition).
  - Positioning: Use absolute positioning. For bottom-right: `bottom: '20px'`, `right: '20px'`. For top-left: `top: '20px'`, `left: '20px'`.
  - Add a very subtle shadow to separate from white backgrounds: `boxShadow: '0 4px 12px rgba(0,0,0,0.15)'`.

- **Whiteboard Pan/Zoom Simulation:**
  - To recreate the whiteboard feel in Remotion, place the screen share asset inside a larger container and animate the `transform: translate(x, y) scale(z)` properties using `spring` or `interpolate` with ease-in-out to move between "zones" of the canvas.

- **Speaker Jump Cuts:**
  - When splitting speaker footage to remove silence, alternate the `transform: scale()` between `1` and `1.05` to create the punch-in effect.

- **Text Overlays:**
  - Use absolute positioning for text over the speaker.
  - Font: A heavy sans-serif (e.g., Inter Black or Roboto Black).
  - Color: `#FFFFFF`.
  - Shadow: `textShadow: '0px 2px 4px rgba(0,0,0,0.5)'` to ensure readability over varying backgrounds.

- **Caption Overlay Strategy (Addition):**
  - Since the original lacks captions, add them in Remotion to improve retention.
  - Position them at the bottom center: `bottom: '40px'`, `left: '50%'`, `transform: 'translateX(-50%)'`.
  - Keep them out of the bottom-right corner to avoid colliding with the PiP.
  - Use a 1-2 line maximum, phrase-level reveal, with a semi-transparent black background box (`backgroundColor: 'rgba(0,0,0,0.6)'`, `padding: '8px 16px'`, `borderRadius: '8px'`) for maximum legibility against the white screen share.
