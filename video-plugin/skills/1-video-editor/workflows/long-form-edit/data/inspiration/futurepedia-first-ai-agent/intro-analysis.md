## Section 9: Key Patterns & Takeaways

1. **100% Hard Cuts:** Zero transitions, cross-dissolves, or fades are used between video clips. Every layout change is an instant hard cut.
2. **The "Slide + PiP" Layout:** Instead of overlaying a PiP on a screen share, the creator builds custom dark-mode slides (`#0B1320` background) and embeds a rounded-rectangle PiP of the speaker (~30-40% width).
3. **No PiP During Screen Share:** When showing actual software (n8n, Gmail), the screen share is 100% full-frame. The speaker is hidden entirely to maximize UI legibility.
4. **Title Card Chapter Markers:** Major sections are separated by 2-second full-screen title cards (white box, black text, dark background).
5. **Speaker as a Bridge:** The full-frame speaker layout is used specifically as a palate cleanser between complex screen shares or slides, usually lasting 5-10 seconds.
6. **Sequential Text Reveal:** On slides, bullet points and definitions pop in sequentially, perfectly synced with the voiceover, rather than appearing all at once.
7. **Zero Audio Clutter:** No background music and no sound effects. The audio track is exclusively a tightly edited, fast-paced voiceover.
8. **No Burned-in Captions:** The video relies on large slide text for visual reinforcement rather than word-by-word captions.
